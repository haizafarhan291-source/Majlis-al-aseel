Our Story

Welcome to our restaurant where tradition meets taste! We serve a wide variety of authentic Indian dishes along with Kerala special mandi, Malabar beef and chicken, aromatic biryanis, and flavorful rice varieties.

Our menu also brings you the best of Arabic cuisine including juicy shawarmas and classic grills, prepared with fresh ingredients and rich spices to give you a true taste of heritage. Whether you crave the warmth of Indian flavors, the richness of Kerala food, or the zest of Arabic delicacies, we promise a dining experience that satisfies every appetite.
